Ballot secrecy &
Targeted in the same sense as Scantegrity/Eperio as the receipts reveal confirmation codes that are
not publicly linkable to candidates without EA/trustee secrets. Additionally, the ZK proofs reveal no ballot choices
beyond the information intentionally opened for audits. \\
Individual verifiability &
Yes: auditors can print-audit codes for their ballot positions and voters can receipt-check that their
recorded selection matches the committed table, with dispute capability if not. \\
Universal verifiability &
Yes: anyone can verify the succinct proof that the committed election table satisfies all protocol
constraints and yields the published tally. \\
Coercion resistance &
Not a goal: like Scantegrity-style receipt systems, Zeeperio provides voter evidence that can be shown to
third parties. We do not claim coercion resistance. \\
