Mask that is $0$ on all of $H$ except possibly at $X=\omega^0$ &
$\mathsf{Mask}_{0}(X)=\displaystyle\frac{\mathsf{Z}_H(X)}{X-\omega^0}$ \\

Mask that is $0$ on all of $H$ except possibly at $X=\omega^{n-1}$ &

Polynomial that is 0 up to padding $[0,\mathcal{P}-1]$ &
$\mathsf{Z}_{\mathsf{head}}(X)=\displaystyle\prod_{i=0}^{\mathcal{P}-1}\left(X-\omega^i\right)$ \\

Polynomial that is 0 for the last $\mathcal{C}$ rows (zeros at $\omega^{n-1},\dots,\omega^{n-\mathcal{C}}$) &
$\mathsf{Mask}_{\mathsf{tail}}(X)=\displaystyle\prod_{t=0}^{\mathcal{C}-1}\left(X-\omega^{n-1-t}\right)$ \\ \hline

Lagrange basis over $H$ used below &
$L_i(X)=\displaystyle\prod_{\substack{0\le j<n\\ j\ne i}}\frac{X-\omega^j}{\omega^i-\omega^j}$ \\

Polynomial with a 1 at the start of every ballot (indices $i\equiv 0\!\!\pmod{\mathcal{C}}$ up to padding) and 0 elsewhere&
$\mathsf{Sel}_{\mathsf{blk}}(X)=\displaystyle\sum_{j=0}^{\lfloor n/\mathcal{C}\rfloor-1} L_{j\mathcal{C}}(X)$ \\

Polynomial that is 1 for all rows belonging to ballot-$b$ ($I_b=\{b\mathcal{C},\dots,b\mathcal{C}+\mathcal{C}-1\}$) and 0 elsewhere &
$\mathsf{Sel}_{b}(X)=\displaystyle\sum_{k=0}^{\mathcal{C}-1} L_{b\mathcal{C}+k}(X)$ \\
