\begin{abstract}

The size of a Zeeperio proof is constant in the number of ballots and voters in an election, as is the time to verify. It is compact enough for verification by a smart contract on a blockchain, such as Ethereum. Zeeperio proofs are `only' linear in the number of candidates, but this seems unavoidable as reading the final tally or checking an audited ballot is linear in the number of candidates. 

\end{abstract}
